Format Type Description
MP4 Video One of the most widely used video formats, supported by most devices and platforms. It provides high-quality video with good compression.
WebM Video A format designed for web use, providing efficient compression and high-quality video, optimized for HTML5 video streaming.
AVI Video An older video format developed by Microsoft, known for high quality but larger file sizes.
MP3 Audio A popular audio format with lossy compression, widely used for music and streaming due to its small file size.
WAV Audio A high-quality, uncompressed audio format commonly used for professional audio recording and editing.
AAC Audio A format that offers better sound quality than MP3 at similar bit rates, commonly used by Apple devices and YouTube.
